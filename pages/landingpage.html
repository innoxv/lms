About Us

Mission

loanSqr provides a transparent, efficient peer-to-peer lending platform. The service connects borrowers directly with investors, offering fair credit access and competitive returns. Advanced technology ensures fast, secure transactions while promoting financial inclusion.

Vision

The vision of loanSqr is to redefine the future of lending by becoming the region’s most trusted and innovative peer-to-peer platform. The goal is to set the standard for financial inclusion, where individuals and businesses can thrive without the constraints of conventional banking systems. Through continuous innovation, exceptional customer service, and strategic partnerships, the platform aims to expand its reach, serving millions of users while maintaining the highest levels of integrity and transparency.

Partnerships

Story

Founded in 2020 by finance and tech experts, loanSqr simplifies lending by cutting out middlemen. Starting small, it has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands. Strategic partnerships with fintech firms and regulators help maintain security and compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about loanSqr.

How do I apply for a loan? ⮟

To apply for a loan, simply register an account, complete your profile, and submit a loan application through our platform. You'll need to provide some basic financial information and the purpose of the loan. Our system will process your application and provide a decision quickly.

What are the requirements to become a lender? ⮟

To become a lender on our platform, you need to be at least 18 years old, have a valid bank account, and complete our identity verification process. There's no minimum amount required to start lending, but we recommend diversifying your investments across multiple loans.

How are interest rates determined? ⮟

Interest rates are determined based on several factors including the borrower's creditworthiness, loan amount, loan term, and current market conditions. Our algorithm assesses risk and suggests rates that are fair to both borrowers and lenders.

Is my personal information secure? ⮟

Yes, we take security very seriously. All personal and financial information is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We comply with all relevant data protection regulations and never share your information with third parties without your consent.

What happens if a borrower defaults? ⮟

We have a collections process in place for late payments. While we can't guarantee against defaults, we carefully screen all borrowers and our platform has historically maintained a low default rate. As a lender, you can further reduce risk by diversifying your investments across multiple loans.
